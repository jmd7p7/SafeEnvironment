Welcome to Volunteer Verify!

Volunteer Verify gives your church the ability to easily track the status of your volunteers' verifications.

Volunteers

Click here to view and edit your volunteers and their verification status. You can add new volunteers or mark existing volunteers as no longer active.

Verifications

Click here to view and edit the verifications for your church. You can add new verifications and delete existing verifications.

Church

Update the information for your church and manage system users in your church who are allowed view access to the program.
